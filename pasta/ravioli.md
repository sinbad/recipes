# Ravioli

Makes 5 batches (2 people)

## Ingredients

### Pasta

* 425g Semolina
* 75g Pasta Flour
* 5 Eggs

### Parma Ham & Emmental Filling

* 500g Emmental
* 4 packs Parma Ham

## Making

* 60g Pasta per side
* 2 sides = 12 Pieces
* 1/2 Tbsp filling per piece
* Usually 2x14pc, 3x16pc portions
* 12-14 sheets = 720-840g pasta

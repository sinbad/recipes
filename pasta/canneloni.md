# Canneloni

Makes 4 portions (x2 people)

## Ingredients

### Pasta

* 250g Semolina
* 50g Pasta Flour (+ more as needed)
* 4 Eggs

### Ricotta & Spinach Filling

* 1160g Spinach
* 500g Ricotta
* 80g Parmesan
* Nutmeg & salt & pepper to taste

## Making

* 60g sheet = 3 canneloni = 1 portion for 1 person
* Need 8 sheets in total (480g pasta)
